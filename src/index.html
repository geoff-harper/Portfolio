My Web Work

SearchBars

SearchBars was a fun side project I came up with for the purpose of learning React. The premise of the web app is to be able to search for bars that serve your favourite craft beer or for your favourite bar’s tap list. Unfortunately, once I got the functionality down with 3 bars in my area and added a few more, I realized that fleshing out the list (and then maintaining it) would be a staggering time commitment for a fun side project. Maybe one day someone will create a Vancouver bar scene API. I crafted the design and UX myself, with a touch of help on the clever name (thanks Alex).

Yellow Pages Site Viewer

This website previewer was something I developed in my spare time at Yellow Pages. Its purpose is to compile screenshots of some sites we created. This was done with vanilla CSS and Javascript so no particularly fancy tech was used. I wanted the aesthetic to simulate flipping through a box of vinyls which I tried to achieve with 3D transforms and box shadows. All of the previews themselves were designed and built by myself on our proprietary WYSIWYG platform. It should be noted that all the previews are drafts and belong to Yellow Pages.

Bodyfuel Supplements

Bodyfuel is one of several handfuls of Wordpress sites I have under my belt. This one in particular has quite a bit of content and a large catalogue of products. The personal fitness vertical also lends itself well to interesting photography which I liked about it. I had a fair bit of leeway on the design of this site. It’s seen some unfortunate revisions since I relinquished control but still reflects my original vision. Tech-wise it’s a pretty standard WP site with some custom written PHP added in to trim up the product catalogues.

Myself

I’m passionate about the web. I spend a boatload of time surfing, cruising, tinkering, or creating on it. Luckily for me, I’m also making a career doing just that. I’ve bounced around between disciplines but feel I have landed on something I really enjoy. The latest web trends and technology are rapidly evolving and keeping up with them is important to me. I strive to make quality work and won’t settle for any less than that. Whether it’s a slick header, a fluid layout, or a little kitten animation, it’s going to be good.

Other than web stuff, I have an affinity for movies, amber ale, local indie rock, and oxford commas. I can usually be found watching Star Wars or stuffing my face with pub food. I’m also a cat guy; it happens.
